## âš¡ 3. Core Mechanism

### The O-Machine (Electrostatic Collision Chamber)

Inside the LDM is an electrostatic chamber that acts like a **regolith â€œpopcorn makerâ€**:

- Dust enters through passive lofting (astronaut activity, natural electrostatic lift, micro-impacts).  
- The chamber fires **randomized multi-directional electrostatic bursts**, suspending grains and forcing high-frequency collisions.  
- Collisions progressively round edges, chip sharp micro-blades, and break micro-adhesion sites.

Over months and years, this reduces the abrasive damage lunar dust inflicts on:

- Suit joints  
- Radiators  
- Seals  
- Optical windows  
- Solar panels  
- Airlock interiors  

Even small reductions have disproportionately large operational benefits.
